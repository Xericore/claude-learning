# Pre-Race Marathon Nutrition Plan

## Daily Carbohydrate Strategy
- **Target: 8-12g carbs per kg body weight** (544-816g daily)
- Focus on easily digestible, high-glycemic carbs in final 3-7 days

## Ideal Foods by Category

### **Carbohydrate Sources** (prioritize these)
- White rice, pasta, bread (refined = easier digestion)
- Potatoes, sweet potatoes
- Oats, rice cereals
- Bananas, dates, dried fruit
- Honey, jam, sports drinks

### **Protein** (moderate, ~1.2-1.6g/kg = 82-109g)
- Lentils, chickpeas, beans
- Tofu, tempeh
- Greek yogurt, cottage cheese
- Eggs (if acceptable)
- Protein powder (pea/rice based)

### **Hydration**
- Water (consistent throughout day)
- Sports drinks with electrolytes (sodium 300-600mg per liter)
- Coconut water
- Herbal tea

## Pre-Race Day (24 hours before)
- Maximize familiar, low-fiber carbs
- Minimize fiber, fat, and excess protein
- Stay hydrated but don't overdo it (causes bathroom issues)

## Race Day (2-3 hours before)
- 200-300g easily digestible carbs
- Example: toast with jam + banana + sports drink

## During Race (if >2.5 hours)
- 30-60g carbs/hour via gels, sports drinks, or dried fruit

# Pre-Competition Nutrition Plan

## Daily Calorie Target
~2,400-2,600 calories (maintenance + light activity buffer)

## Meal Structure
**4-5 smaller meals** spaced 2-3 hours apart to minimize digestive stress

---

## Sample Daily Breakdown

### Breakfast (6-7 AM)
- White toast with almond butter
- Banana
- Orange juice
- ~500 cal

### Mid-Morning (9 AM)
- Rice cakes with honey
- Applesauce
- ~250 cal

### Lunch (12 PM)
- Grilled chicken breast (150g)
- White rice (1.5 cups cooked)
- Carrots (cooked)
- Olive oil drizzle
- ~550 cal

### Pre-Swim Snack (1.5-2 hrs before)
- Energy drink or sports drink (6% carbs)
- Banana
- ~200 cal

### Dinner (6 PM)
- Lean fish or chicken (150g)
- White pasta or rice
- Well-cooked vegetables (zucchini, peeled potatoes)
- ~550 cal

### Evening (optional)
- Rice cakes + jam or honey
- ~200 cal

✓ **High carbs** (55-60%) for energy

✓ **Low fat** - avoid fried foods  
✓ **Hydrate well** - 3-4L water daily  
✓ **Avoid** within 3 hours of swimming: dairy, high fiber, excessive fat
